Utilize computer vision to determine the predictive utility of non-suicidal self-injuries using tissue damage and lethality as severity signals derived from clinical self-injury images in predicting prospective suicide attempt risk. In collaboration with Harvard University and Massachusetts General Hospital.

Technologies Used: Detectron2, RNN, Pytorch, opencv.

Feature Pyramid Networks for Detection. Faster-RCNN, MaskRCNN for segmentation. Processed and cleaned clinical data annotations (n=16000).
  • Analyzing the number of participants vs. accuracy graph, also working on hGRU-FPN for segmentation of scars.
  • Participated in over 100 hours of IRB training to gain access to REDCap and the MGH Data.
